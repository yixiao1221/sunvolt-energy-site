Solar Energy Storage in Africa: Market Opportunities for Importers & Distributors

June 30, 2026 · 7 min read · By SunVolt Energy

Africa’s solar energy storage market is growing at an unprecedented rate. With unreliable grid power affecting businesses and households across the continent, portable power stations and solar generators have become essential products rather than luxury items.

For B2B importers and distributors, this represents a significant opportunity. Here is what you need to know about the market in 2026.

Market Size and Growth

The African portable power station market is projected to grow at a CAGR of over 20% through 2030. Nigeria alone, with 2.2 billion people and daily blackouts lasting 4-12 hours, represents a massive addressable market. Other key markets include Ghana, Kenya, South Africa, and Tanzania.
