FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from our B2B partners

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?▼
Our MOQ is 10 units for standard products. For OEM/ODM orders, the MOQ is 50-100 units depending on the customization requirements. We also offer sample orders (1-2 units) for quality testing before bulk commitment.
What payment terms do you offer?▼
We require 50% deposit to start production, with the 50% balance due before shipment. Payment methods include T/T bank transfer, Western Union, and Alibaba Trade Assurance.
How long does shipping take?▼
Sample orders ship within 3-5 working days via DHL/FedEx (3-7 days). Bulk orders take 15-25 days for production. Sea freight to Africa/Middle East typically takes 20-30 days. Air freight is also available.
Do you provide OEM/private label services?▼
Yes. We offer full OEM/ODM services including custom branding, packaging design, product specifications, and user manuals in your language. Your brand, your product.
What certifications do your products have?▼
All our products are CE, FCC, and ROHS certified. We also provide ISO 9001 factory audits. Additional certifications for specific markets can be arranged.
