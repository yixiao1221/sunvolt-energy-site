Who We Are

SunVolt Energy is a Foshan-based supplier specializing in portable power stations, solar generators, and energy storage solutions. We partner with ISO 9001 and CE-certified manufacturers to deliver reliable, affordable power products to markets worldwide.

Founded by a team with 15+ years of experience in the building materials and energy product export industry, we understand the quality standards and service expectations of international B2B buyers.

Our mission: Make reliable backup power accessible and affordable for everyone, especially in regions where electricity is not always available.
