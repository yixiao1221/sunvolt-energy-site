Warranty Details

What Is Covered

  • Manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship
  • Battery cells that fail to hold charge or degrade below 80% capacity
  • Inverter/charger circuit board failures under normal use
  • LCD display and control panel malfunctions
  • AC/DC output port failures
  • Charging adapter and cable defects

Not Covered

  • Damage from misuse, accident, or improper installation
  • Water damage / liquid ingress (unless IP-rated model)
  • Unauthorized repairs or modifications
  • Normal wear and tear (cosmetic)
  • Force majeure events
  • Consumable items (fuses, filters)

Support FAQ

Common Support Questions

How do I start a warranty claim?▼
Contact us on WhatsApp with your order number and a description of the issue. Our team will respond within 1 hour to diagnose and provide a solution. Have your product serial number ready (found on the bottom of the unit).
Do I need to return the faulty unit?▼
In most cases, no. We will diagnose the issue and send replacement parts or provide repair instructions. For complete unit replacements, we may request return for quality analysis at our expense.
What is the response time for technical support?▼
WhatsApp: within 1 hour (business hours GMT+8). Email: within 4 hours. For urgent issues, WhatsApp is the fastest channel.
Do you provide on-site support?▼
For large distributor partners, we offer remote technical support and training. On-site support can be arranged for container-level orders. Contact us for details.

Need Immediate Help?

Our support team is ready to assist you. We typically respond within 1 hour.
